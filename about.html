THE STORY

Where ideas
become products

UDBHAV'26 was born from a simple belief — students deserve more than textbook problems. They deserve the pressure of real deadlines, real stakeholders, and real consequences. This hackathon strips away everything comfortable and asks one question: can you build something that matters in 24 hours?

Organized by Enclope (Alta School of Technology) in collaboration with Sunstone, and hosted at Sage University, Indore, UDBHAV'26 brings together the brightest minds across colleges and disciplines. 50 teams. 10 domains. One winner.

High-Intensity Format
24 hours of continuous building across 4 structured phases — from PS drop to grand finale.
PS Drop
10 problem statements release simultaneously. Teams pick their domain and lock in — no going back.
Expert Mentorship
Industry mentors and judges providing real-time guidance, direction pivots, and strategic feedback.
₹45,000 Prize Pool
Significant rewards for the winners, runners-up, and domain-specific recognitions.
